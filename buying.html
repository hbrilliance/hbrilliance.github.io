1. How do I pay?

Payments may be made either with Wire Transfer, Major Credit Card, or Paypal. Fees for Credit Cards and Paypal will be added to the amount during checkout.

2. What is the return policy?

Harrison's Brilliance encourages all buyers to be sure of model prior to purchase. We will honor returns within 48 hours of receiving if the product is not as described. We do not accept returns for “Buyer’s Remorse”.

3. How do you ship?

Harrison's Brilliance ships all packages overnight domestically and Priority (3-5 days) internationally. Shipping charge is added to order at checkout. This charge is per order and NOT per item. We do not ship to Mexico, Russia, or Korea.
